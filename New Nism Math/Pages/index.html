Our Top Creators of the Month

Socratica

Channel, Website

Socratica provides a valuable online learning platform that provides niche, yet accessible learning content pertaining to mathematics. Topics include Abstract Algebra, Astronomy, Calculus, Biology, Chemistry, Programming, and many more. Socratica teaches multiple hard-to-find topics in the uOttawa Mathematics Program.

Organic Chemistry Tutor

YouTube, Website

The Organic Chemistry Tutor is a household name for all students in STEM. Covering topics such as Organic Chemistry, Chemistry, Calculus, Physics, and many more. To say that is content provides overwhelming value to introductory courses is an understatement. He covers top to bottom, for every topic, and for nearly every first year class.

Wrath of Math

Channel, Patreon

Wrath of Math is a popular YouTube channel dedicated to delivering high-quality math tutorials and educational content. Known for his passion for mathematics and teaching, Wrath of Math offers clear and insightful explanations on a wide range of mathematical topics. His videos cover subjects such as Calculus, Linear Algebra, Differential Equations, Abstract Algebra, and more.
